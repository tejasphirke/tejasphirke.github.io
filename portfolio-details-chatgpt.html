Project Details

This project showcases the analysis on chatgpt and compared with AI which is Bing. Based on the time of the release and version mentioned for chatgpt and bing compared the average sentiment for different versions of chatgpt and Bing. There is a major trend that is seen in chatgpt and reviews for chatgpt are mostly positive and for bing even for the latest version sentiments come out to be average.
